All about Cricket

Cricket is a sport enjoyed by billions of people around the world. Read on to discover the rich history of this beautiful game.



Origins of Cricket:

Cricket is believed to have originated from England, where the earliest records of cricket being played was around the 13th Century! However, cricket has evolved drstically since then. In the 1700s, cricket was rapidly gaining popularity and it soon became a more serious sport. Thus, it needed its own set of governing rules.



The famous Marylebone Cricket Club was founded in 1877 at Lord's and became the custodian for the sport's laws. Marylebone Cricket Club to this day are the ones responsible for revvising and writing cricket rules.
